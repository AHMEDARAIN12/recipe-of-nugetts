Eassy Recipe for nuggets

ingridents

  • chiken breast(cut into bite size pieces)3-4
  • ketchup 4tbsp
  • oil 1 tbsp
  • brown sugar 1 tbs
  • white viniger 2 tbsp
  • soya soauce 3tbsp
  • salt 1/2tbsp
  • chiken powder 1tbsp
  • mixture of 1 tsbp cold water 2tsbp corn flour
  • sesame seed for granishing

method

  • wishk all the ingridients(execpt the corn flour mixture) togeather in bowl and add the chiken pieces to the mixture
  • prepare the corn flour by mixing it with water
  • coat the chiken with corn flour and transfer it onto a large pan drizzled with very little oil saute the chiken at medium heat for about four minutes or until it becomes brown in colour
  • once fried ,transfer the chiken to a baking dish and bake it for about 20 to 25 minutes until it is cooked through
  • garnish the shots with seasame seeds and serve it alongisde adollop of coleslaw
